Poems by Yulia

Poem 1: The Rookie Coder

In a world of ones and zeros, they dared to roam,
The rookie coder, in their basement, found a home.
With HTML and CSS, they took the first dive,
But soon realized, it was harder than staying alive!

They battled with bugs, like knights of old,
Syntax errors and typos, their stories were told.
Their friends said, "Just quit, it's not worth the strife,"
But they laughed and replied, "I'll code for life!"

Poem 2: The Code Confounder

In a land of divs and semicolons, they took a stand,
The code confounder, with mouse in hand.
They clicked and they clacked, with a puzzled frown,
CSS made their brain spin 'round and 'round!

JavaScript's quirks, like a mischievous elf,
Made their code dance, all by itself.
They sighed and they groaned, with each bug they met,
But they knew in their heart, they'd get it yet!

Poem 3: The Web Wizard Wannabe

In the realm of web dev, they aspired to reign,
The web wizard wannabe, with a passion insane.
They learned of frameworks and libraries galore,
But React and Vue left them wanting more!

They Googled and Googled, from dusk till dawn,
For answers to problems that seemed to spawn.
But despite all the hurdles, they stayed on track,
For they knew that web dev was never a cakewalk!

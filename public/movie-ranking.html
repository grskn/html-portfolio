The Best Movies According to Yulia Ge

My top 3 movies of all-time:

What Dreams May Come

"What Dreams May Come" is a poignant 1998 film starring Robin Williams and Annabella Sciorra. It tells the story of Chris Nielsen, who embarks on a breathtaking journey through the afterlife to reunite with his wife, Annie. The film explores themes of love, life, and death, featuring visually stunning landscapes and showcasing the enduring power of love that transcends mortality.

War Dogs

"War Dogs" is a 2016 film directed by Todd Phillips, starring Jonah Hill and Miles Teller. It's a darkly comedic and thrilling exploration of two young men who become unlikely arms dealers, exploiting government contracts to supply weapons to American forces in Iraq and Afghanistan. As their ambitions grow, they navigate a morally ambiguous world where friendship and ethics are put to the test. The film is based on a true story and provides an entertaining and thought-provoking look into the world of international arms dealing.

Fracture

"Fracture" is a 2007 legal thriller starring Anthony Hopkins and Ryan Gosling. The film centers on the intense battle between a brilliant engineer, Ted Crawford (Hopkins), and a determined prosecutor, Willy Beachum (Gosling), as they engage in a high-stakes courtroom showdown following an attempted murder. Hopkins delivers a mesmerizing performance in this suspenseful exploration of deception and justice.
